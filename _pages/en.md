# Lodging

We recommend staying in North or Central Tel Aviv. Airbnb is a good option, or there are a number of international hotels in this area.

# Travel

Arrive at Ben Gurion Airport.

From there, you can take a train from terminal 3 to Tel Aviv (use the Israel Railways app for tickets).

You can also take a taxi (we recommend the Gett app).

# Henna Ceremony

A Henna is a traditional Morrocan-Jewish engagement ceremony. Expect food, dancing and henna!

# Wedding

The wedding will be a traditional Jewish ceremony. Connor and Natalie will sign a Ketubah, meet under a Chuppah and break a glass. More eating and dancing to follow!

## Attire

Tel Aviv in Summer is hot! Wear something comfortable. Light fabrics are your friend, and suit jackets are not required.
